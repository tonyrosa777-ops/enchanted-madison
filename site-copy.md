## Policies & Stay Information

### Check-In & Check-Out
- **Check-In:** 4:00 PM
- **Check-Out:** 10:00 AM
- Self check-in property. No staffed front desk.
- Check-in instructions and access codes sent 48 hours prior to arrival.
- Unauthorized late check-out: $75 flat fee.

### Reservations & Payment
- 100% of reservation total collected at time of booking.
- Invoices available upon request.
- Primary booking guest must be **25 years of age or older** and must remain on property for duration of stay.
- Valid government-issued ID may be required.

### Security Deposit (Direct Bookings Only)
- $300 refundable hold placed on card 48 hours prior to arrival.
- Released within 3 days after checkout, pending inspection.
- May be applied toward: damage, missing items, excessive cleaning, smoking violations, hot tub misuse, unauthorized guests, late check-out, or any breach of house policies.
- Airbnb/OTA reservations follow platform damage protection process.

### Cancellation Policy

**Cottages, Glamping Tents, Hot Tub Experiences & All Accommodations (Except Campsites):**
- 30+ days before arrival → 100% refund
- 14–29 days before arrival → 50% refund
- Less than 14 days → Non-refundable

**Tent Camping Sites:**
- 14+ days before arrival → 100% refund
- 7–13 days before arrival → 50% refund
- Less than 7 days → Non-refundable

Add-on experiences follow the same cancellation terms as the associated lodging reservation. Reschedule requests reviewed case-by-case. No refunds for early departures or unused nights after check-in. Weather/power outages do not qualify for refunds unless property is uninhabitable.

### Quiet Hours
**10:00 PM – 8:00 AM** strictly enforced.
Applies to: music, outdoor areas, hot tubs, decks, lounges, elevated conversation, and barking pets.

### No Party Policy
Strictly enforced. Prohibited: exceeding occupancy, unapproved celebrations, excessive noise, commercial activity without consent. Violations result in immediate removal without refund.

### Hot Tub Policy
- Guests under 18 strictly prohibited.
- Shower before entering.
- No oils, soaps, bath bombs, or additives.
- No glass in or around the hot tub.
- Cover must be secured after each use.
- Temperature must be reset to 99°F at checkout.
- Misuse requiring service: $250 flat fee.

### Smoking Policy
- Smoking prohibited within 50 feet of any glamping tent or enclosed accommodation.
- Outdoor smoking only beyond 50 feet.
- All materials must be fully extinguished and properly disposed of.
- Violations: $250 flat fee.

### Guest Conduct
- Guests must remain appropriately clothed in all outdoor and shared areas.
- Conduct visible to neighbors, other guests, or exterior cameras must remain lawful and respectful.
- Inappropriate or disruptive behavior may result in removal without refund.

### Visitors
- Maximum 2 day visitors with prior written approval.
- Visitors must depart before quiet hours (10:00 PM).
- Total occupancy limits must never be exceeded.
- Unauthorized visitors may result in immediate removal without refund.

### Surveillance
- Exterior areas and shared spaces may be monitored by 24/7 video surveillance.
- No cameras inside private accommodations or hot tub areas.
- Tampering with cameras: grounds for immediate eviction without refund.

### Housekeeping
- Professionally cleaned prior to arrival.
- Daily housekeeping not included unless arranged in advance.
- Guests agree to dispose of trash properly, avoid moving furniture, and report accidental damage promptly.
